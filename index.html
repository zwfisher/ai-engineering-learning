Survival kit

  • Protect the build block, sacrifice fragments first. If a week shrinks, drop the videos — you can catch them on your phone later — but keep the one hands-on block. Building beats watching.
  • End each build block with one sentence: "where I am / what's next" in a notes file. With interruptions, future-you won't remember, and this saves the re-orientation time that kills fragmented learning.
  • Use L&D time for the heavy-video weeks (4–6, 8–10, 13–15). Lectures are easy to justify and easy to pause.
  • Free to audit ≠ free certificate. Coursera and DeepLearning.AI courses give you full video and most labs for free; only the certificate costs money. If you just need the skills, auditing is enough.
  • Nothing expires. The plan is sequential, not date-locked. Sick kid or work crunch? Slide it all forward and pick up where you left off.
